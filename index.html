Niagara Grape and Wine Festival Case Study

Team Grapevine partnered with the Nigara-based non-for-profit association, The Niagara Grape and Wine Festival to explore ways in which a website redesign could help the NGWF achieve increased site traffic, overall awareness, and event attendance.

The Festival

Located in St. Catharines, Ontario, The Niagara Grape and Wine Festival is the oldest wine festival in North America. Established in 1951, the Festival is a non-for-profit association dedicated to the promotion of the grape growing, winemaking, and tourism culture of Niagara. This ‘festival of festivals’ brings over 150,000 attendees to Niagara every year and is a major economic driver for the region. Steeped in tradition and pride, the NGWF is an integral community festival and association in Niagara.

Research

Problem Statement: The NGWF was established in 1951 as a collective non -profit to promote the wineries and grape growers of the region with a vision to make the world “fall in love with Niagara Wine Country”. We have observed that the NGWF website isn’t reaching an audience broad enough to capture new, or out-of-town consumers, diminishing the investment individual stakeholders (wineries) are willing to invest into the collective organization. How might we improve the NGWF website so that new and out-of-town customers are drawn to the magic of what NGWF has to offer, bringing much needed economic support to the industry, increasing winery visibility, increasing attendance to events, and ultimately boosting the profitability of the Niagara wine region. .

Iterations

Original NGWF navigation caused user confusion due to duplicate links and inconsistency of button labeling This caused friction between users and their set out goals, potentially resulting in abandoned task completion The sitemap redesign aimed to reduce the difficulty users faced while navigating the site by emaligimating duplicate links, and organizing primary and secondary navigation into a more instinctual way that reflected the NGO’s event structure Stakeholder feedback made it more clear as to how the events and passes are structured seasonally, and this informed our final iteration
